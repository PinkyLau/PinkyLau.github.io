Egg Pudding Recipe

Ingredients And Kitchenware To Make Egg Pudding:

Kitchenware

  • Mixing bowl
  • Whisk or chopsticks for mixingm
  • 4 tinfoil bowls or air fryer-safe bowls (add more bowls if needed)

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 10g sugar
  • 150g milk
  • Sugar for topping

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, whisk together two eggs, sugar, and milk. Make sure not to over-whisk it to lessen the bubbles and foam. You can remove air bubbles and foam using a sieve or spoon.


  2. Next, prepare your reusable tin foil bowls. Alternatively, you can use air fryer-safe bowls that are ceramic, metal, glass, or cast iron. Oven-safe bowls can also be used in an air fryer. Then, pour the egg mixture into the bowls.


  3. Bake in an air fryer at 160°C (320°F) for 10 minutes.


  4. After 10 minutes, take it out and sprinkle some sugar on top. This step is needed if you want a beautiful brown glaze on top.


  5. Bake for an additional 5 minutes. The top should be golden brown and slightly charred. Serve while hot and crisp!
